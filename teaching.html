Courses for Academic year 2020 - 2021

MATH3416 Complex Analysis (Semester 2) [HKBU SSOid login required]

Description: This course provides an up-to-date introduction to the basic theory of analytic functions of one complex variable. Residue Theorem and its applications to the evaluation of integrals and sums will be one of the main objectives. Also conformal mappings and their applications will be discussed.

MATH1025 Introduction to Mathematics and Statistics (Semester 1) [HKBU SSOid login required]

Description: This course introduces basic mathematics and statistics for university students. The mathematics part covers elementary calculus, optimization, numerical methods and applications in finance, while the statistics part explains basic statistical notions, including confidence intervals, tests of hypotheses and linear regression, and discusses misuse and abuse of statistics.
