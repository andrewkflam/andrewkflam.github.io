MATH3416 Complex Analysis (Semester 2)

Description: This course provides an up-to-date introduction to the basic theory of analytic functions of one complex variable. Cauchy's theorems and formulas, Residue Theorem and its applications to the evaluation of integrals will be the main objectives. Also conformal mappings and their applications will be discussed.

MATH1025 Introduction to Mathematics and Statistics (Semester 1)

Description: This course introduces basic mathematics and statistics for university students. The mathematics part covers elementary calculus, optimization, numerical methods and applications in finance, while the statistics part explains basic statistical notions, including confidence intervals, tests of hypotheses and linear regression, and discusses misuse and abuse of statistics.
